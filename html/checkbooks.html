TO USE OUR SIMULATOR:

  • Select your library's location and the length of your financial projection from the dropdowns provided.
  • Enter the maximum expected government contributions above the baseline, as well as the maximum expected shortfall. Shortfall should be entered as a negative number. "1" represents a $1,000,000 contribution, ".3" represents a $300,000 contribution, "-1" represents a $1,000,000 shortfall, etc.
  • Enter the maximum expected private contributions above the baseline, as well as the maximum expected shortfall.
  • Click "Calculate" to tabulate your results.
